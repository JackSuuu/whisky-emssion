Scotch Whiskey: A Legacy Driving Net Zero


Economic Impact

Scotch whisky is more than just a spirit, it is a symbol of Scotland's heritage and a key driver of its economy. In 2022, the Scotch whisky industry contributed £7.1 billion in gross value added to the UK economy and accounted for 74% of Scotland's food and drink exports in 2023.

Commitment to Sustainability

In line with global sustainability efforts, the Scotch whisky industry has set an ambitious goal to achieve Net Zero emissions across Scope 1, 2, and 3 by 2040

Our Story: Innovating for a Greener Future

Our team is pioneering in developing an Al-driven carbon calculator to streamline and simplify the process of assessing the industry's carbon footprint enabling whisky producers to more efficiently work toward this sustainability goal

Our Purpose: Harnessing Big Data to Track Carbon Footprint


1. Standardise calculations

The calculator continously simplifies the input process, allowing producers to enter basic data like energy use or transport information easily. With AI handling the complex calculations, even smaller distilleries can access their carbon impact without needing technical expertise.

2. Simplify inputs

The tool could provide actionable insights for reducuing emissions. It identifies carbon hotspots in production and suggests strategies tailored to the whisky industry, helping producers make informed decisions to lower their environmental impact

3. Guide emssion reduction

Our AI-driven carbon calculator ensures consistent carbon footprint assessments by standardizing emmisions calcualtions across Scope 1, 2 and 3. The appproach enables producers and investors to accurately compare and track their carbon impact. Fostering transparency and industry-wide best practices.
